# 🌟 Welcome to the Enigma Personalisation Engine

The Enigma Engine transforms your current state into timeless wisdom. Follow this simple guide to begin your journey.

---

### 🟢 Step 1: The Daily Reflection (Your Input)
Instead of throwing coins, you now use **5 Sliders** to check-in with yourself.
1. **Energy**: How is your physical vitality?
2. **Clarity**: Is your mind sharp or foggy?
3. **Emotional**: Are you feeling balanced or turbulent?
4. **Social**: How are your current connections?
5. **Direction**: Do you have a clear goal today?

**💡 Pro Tip:** If you aren't sure about a slider, leave it at **3**. This signals a "transition" and triggers a **Changing Line**.

---

### 🔵 Step 2: State Encoding (The Magic)
The engine converts your slider values into a **6-bit Binary String** (0s and 1s):
- **1 (Yang)**: High resonance (Slider value 3, 4, or 5)
- **0 (Yin)**: Low resonance (Slider value 1 or 2)

The system then scans all 64 I Ching hexagrams to find the one that best matches your "Energy Profile."

---

### 🟡 Step 3: Reading the Oracle (The Insight)
The Oracle provides a multi-dimensional response:
- **The Master's Insight**: The core message of your hexagram.
- **The Dance of Qi**: How your specific state (Wood, Fire, Earth, etc.) influences the reading.
- **Strategic Action**: Practical steps you can take *right now*.

---

### 🟠 Step 4: Refine the Engine (Your Feedback)
After the reading, tell the system how it did:
1. **Relevant?**: Did the theme match your situation?
2. **Clarity?**: Rate it with 1 to 3 stars.
3. **Action?**: Did you decide to do something based on the advice?

**♻️ Why this matters:** Your feedback updates your **Personal Weight Profile**. If you mark a reading as relevant, the engine will "remember" that this hexagram works well for your current state.

---

### 🔴 Step 5: Track Your Growth
Visit the **Analytics Dashboard** (`dashboard.py`) to see:
- Your weekly clarity trends.
- Which hexagrams have been most helpful.
- How often you've taken action on the Oracle's advice.

---

### 🧘 Remember:
This is a **Decision Support Tool**, not a fortune teller. Use it to reflect on your current state and find balance.
