# 🌈 Your Magic Wisdom Guide (Junior Edition)

Welcome! This system is like a **Wise Friend** or a **Magic Mirror** that helps you understand your feelings and gives you cool advice. Here is how to use it!

---

### 🟢 Step 1: How are you feeling? (The Sliders)
Move the sliders to show how you feel right now. 
1. **Power**: Do you have lots of energy to run, or are you sleepy?
2. **Brain**: Is your head clear, or does it feel like a messy room?
3. **Heart**: Are you happy and calm, or a bit grumpy?
4. **Friends**: Did you have fun talking to people today?
5. **Goal**: Do you know what you want to do next?

**⭐ Secret Trick:** If you don't know, put the slider in the **Middle (Number 3)**. This tells the computer that things are changing!

---

### 🔵 Step 2: The Secret Code
The computer takes your feelings and turns them into a **Secret Pattern**. It looks at 64 different "Life Stories" and picks the one that matches your mood today. It's like finding the perfect puzzle piece for your day!

---

### 🟡 Step 3: Listen to the Teacher
The "Oracle" is like a very wise teacher. It will tell you:
- **A Story**: What your pattern means.
- **Nature's Lesson**: How things like Water, Fire, or Mountains can teach you.
- **Your Mission**: A fun and easy task you can do to have a better day.

---

### 🟠 Step 4: Help the Computer Learn
After you read the advice, tell the computer if it was right!
1. **Did it fit?**: Click **Yes** if it felt like it was talking about your day.
2. **Stars**: Give it 1, 2, or 3 stars if it helped you think clearly.
3. **Action**: Did you do your mission?

**🎈 Why?**: When you give stars, the computer learns more about you. It gets smarter and gives even better advice next time!

---

### 🔴 Step 5: See Your Journey
Look at your **Journey Map** to see how your "Heart" and "Brain" scores change every week. It's like a video game where you level up your wisdom!

---

### 🧘 Just Remember:
This is a tool to help you **think and be happy**. It's not a fortune teller—it's a way to help you be the best "you" possible!
